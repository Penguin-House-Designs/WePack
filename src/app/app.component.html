About Us



WePack moving & logistics is your preferred local, national, and international moving company based out of Park City, Utah. Since 1997, WePack has worked hard to provide industry-leading moving and relocation services.



WePack oversees a nationwide network of highly trained independent providers who understand that a successful move is more than just delivering a client's possessions to their final destination. We earn the trust of our valued clients by creating an unparalleled comfort level during the moving process while at the same time understanding the importance of being accountable in every facet of the service delivery process.
