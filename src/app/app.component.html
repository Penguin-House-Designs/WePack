About Us



WePack moving & logistics is your preferred local, national, and international relocation expert, a reputation we have worked hard to earn and consistently maintain for the last 20 years. Since 1997 WePack has provided industry-leading moving and relocation services.


Based out of Park City, Utah, WePack oversees a nationwide network of highly trained independent providers. WePack understands that a successful move is more than just delivering a client's possessions to their final destination. We earn the trust of our valued clients by creating an unparalleled comfort level during the moving process while at the same time understanding the importance of being accountable in every facet of the service delivery process.

Services

Packing & Unpacking



Why trust two guys from the phonebook when you can trust us and our highly trained, fast-pace team to do it for you.

Moving



Managing thousands of projects nationally and internationally a year, WePack is committed to seamless logistics when it comes to relocating. This is allows clients a stress-free experience.

Supplies



WePack prides itself on being a green business. We will provide our clients with recycled supplies or new supplies depending on their preference.

Storage (short-term & long-term)



We understand storage may be needed when it comes to relocating. Storage arrangements can be easily met along with the necessary security levels. WePack has partnered with high standard storage facilities that can also cater to cars or other large vehicles as well.

Crating & Uncrating



If cardboard boxes don't cut it for some of your home's treasures, then crating and uncrating services can be provided onsite and off.

Vehicle Transportation



Arranged transport of cars, SUVs, and other vehicles can be done in a timely manner. Transportation can be included in custom programs.

Fine Art Removal & Installation



Specialized artwork removal, transportation/protection and reinstallation can be included.

Home Staging



The job doesn't end when your belongings are delivered. At your request, our team can assemble your furniture and move them to the desired locations. Our staging services provide a seemless and stress-free transition into your new home.

Piano Transport



Pianos are expensive pieces of equipment that require the upmost care when it comes to moving. WePack has partnerned with the best piano movers in the industry and also offers storage for these instruments in secure, climate controlled facilities.
