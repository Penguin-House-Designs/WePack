About Us



WePack moving & logistics is your preferred local, national, and international relocation expert, a reputation we have worked hard to earn and consistently maintain for the last 20 years. Since 1997 WePack has provided industry-leading moving and relocation services.


Based out of Park City, Utah, WePack oversees a nationwide network of highly trained independent providers. WePack understands that a successful move is more than just delivering a client's possessions to their final destination. We earn the trust of our valued clients by creating an unparalleled comfort level during the moving process while at the same time understanding the importance of being accountable in every facet of the service delivery process.
