About Us



WePack is a safe and secure way to relocate you comfortably to wherever you need to be. With over 20 years of expertise, WePack provides moving service that provides all-inclusive amenities from eco-conscious, recycled packing supplies to art transportation/re-installation to private car transportation nationwide. Moving internationally? No problem. Our services can be customized to our clients’ specific needs.


Based out of Park City, Utah, WePack offers consultations to customize packing, moving and/or transportation programs. Priding ourselves on flexibility and driven by specified client needs, our company has partnered with a collection of high-end, quality independent agents to deliver exceptional service.
